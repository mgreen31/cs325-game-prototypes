Blown Away

  • Originality: What is original about your game? - This game allows the player to become a bird and avoid getting blown away in wind storm.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology? The gravity changes variably and randomly every few seconds, forcing the player to change their strategy on the fly.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? I learned about physics in phaser, mainly how to update gravities in different directions, as well as how to create gamestates that share resources. What was the easiest or hardest part of making it? The hardest part of this prototype was understanding the relationship between gamestates and ensuring that appropriate variables are reset when switching between them. The easiest part was implementing the movement of the player and interactible objects.
